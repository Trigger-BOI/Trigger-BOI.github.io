The size of the ego

A big ego = irrational self-confidence or irrational self-doubt.

Is there such a thing as too small an ego? It depends on the person. It can lead to a lack of self-love.

Is being ego-less a good thing? I don't think so. Why would you keep living if you are nothing anymore? You need to gain control over your ego

Saying that you have a small ego is a sign of a big ego.

Being humble is a sign of a small ego.

What does it mean to gain control over it?
