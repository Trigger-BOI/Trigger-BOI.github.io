The Highest Form of Love

I don't believe that love for a person is the highest form of love. I think there is something even greater, something much more important: the love for absolute beauty in everything and everyone. Love for a person comes with no guarantees. My partner could leave, pass away, or the love could simply fade. The beauty in things and people, however, is endless. There will always be beauty, and this is what we should truly pursue. Still, I believe that love for a person, like a girlfriend or wife, holds significant value. For absolute happiness, I think there needs to be a combination of both. The love for beauty is a less intense but constant form of love once you recognize it, while love for a person is a much more intense love. This is the love you feel the most strongly, but it often fluctuates.

Therefore, a combination of the two is needed.
